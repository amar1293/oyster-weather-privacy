Privacy Policy for Oyster Weather

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Oyster Weather ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

To provide accurate weather forecasts, Oyster Weather may collect:

  • Location data — your device's GPS or approximate location, used to show weather for your current location or searched locations.
  • Device and usage data — device type, operating system, app version, and basic usage/crash diagnostics, used to maintain and improve the App.
  • Saved preferences — settings such as favorite locations or units (metric/imperial), stored locally on your device or with your account if you create one.

We do not collect more information than is necessary to provide and improve the App's functionality.

How We Use Information

  • To provide current and forecasted weather information for your location.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App.
  • To diagnose technical issues and understand general usage patterns.

Third-Party Services

Oyster Weather may rely on third-party weather data providers and standard app-platform services (such as Apple App Store or Google Play analytics and crash reporting) to operate. These providers process limited data as needed to deliver their services and are bound by their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except as required to operate the App (e.g., sending your location to a weather data provider to retrieve a forecast) or as required by law.

Data Retention

Location data is used to fetch weather information and is not retained longer than necessary to provide that function. Saved preferences persist until you delete the App or clear its data.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access at any time through your device's settings; the App will then require manual location entry.
  • You can delete the App at any time to remove locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Oyster Weather is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
